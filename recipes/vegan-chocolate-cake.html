Vegan Chocolate Cake

Description

This vegan cake is a really simple, yet very tasty cake.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup water
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon distilled white vinegar

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  3. Sift flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.
  4. Add water, oil, vanilla, and vinegar, and mix them together until smooth.
  5. Pour the mixture into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake the mixture until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out come (for about 45min).
  7. Serve the cake with berries.
